Pineapple Pizza

Description

The pizza with the perfect combination of acid, fat and heat.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1.5 Cup pizza sauce
  • 1.5 - 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1.5 cup fresh diced pineapple, thoroughly patted dry
  • Dried oregano to taste
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • Salt to taste

Instruction

  1. Make sure your oven is preheated at 475 degrees F.
  2. After you have brushed the dough with olive oil and pricked it with a fork, add the toppings. Start pizza sauce, then the cheese, ham, pineapple, and oregano.
